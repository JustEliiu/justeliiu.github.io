About

I'm Elisha Deeshan — a graphic designer, photographer, and videographer based in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

I'm a graphic designer and visual creative with experience in branding, print design, social media content, photography, and video editing. Currently working at Ministry of Crab, I create visual experiences that help brands connect with their audience through thoughtful design and compelling storytelling.

I'm passionate about creating work that not only looks great but leaves a lasting impression. Outside of design, I'm building my own streetwear brand, exploring photography and videography, and constantly finding new ways to tell stories through visuals.
