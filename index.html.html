About

I'm Elisha Deeshan — a graphic designer, photographer, and videographer based in Colombo, Sri Lanka.

I currently lead creative at Ministry of Crab, handling visual identity, print, social content, and photography across multiple venues. My work lives at the intersection of brand strategy and aesthetic craft.

I care equally about how something functions and how it makes you feel. Beyond client work, I'm building my own streetwear brand and chasing the perfect shot on four wheels.
